The homeoptima Team

Before finding HomeOptima, Ashish had served the corporate world for over 15 years across a wide spectrum of domains including Technology, SaaS, and Consumer Goods.

With HomeOptima, Ashish stays committed to applying his 15 years of experience in business intelligence, complex problem solving and optimization strategies to improve the quality of decisions in the residential real estate marketplace through innovative and user-friendly decision tools for both homebuyers and realtors.

Ashish holds a Masters’ Degree in Applied Sciences, Industrial & Systems Engineering from University of Guelph, a Masters’ Degree in Management Sciences from University of Waterloo, and an MBA from Kellogg School of Management.

He made his home in Mississauga and lives with his wife and two beautiful children.
