How it works - home Selling with homeoptima

7-Step Proven Home Selling Process

Duration 1-2 weeks

Step 1

Get to know your situation and your home

Selling time frame, upgrades, square footage, are you upsizing, utility equipment, appliances, roof, structures etc.

Step 2

Get to know your situation and your home

A proven foolproof plan to sell your home to top dollars, quickly and hassle free. Plan includes buying and selling scenarios based on equity in your current home, closing costs and additional savings that you may have.

Step 3

Home Prep (decluttering / staging / cleaning)

We offer 25 free decluttering / home preparation hours to prepare you home. Services include, light staging, decluttering and deep cleaning. We also provide free full staging estimate. For more information, check out our services page.

Step 4

Marketing

Our Marketing plan uses a unique combination of media, optimized listing keywords, pricing strategy and promoting your home to 30 different online and print sources as well as introducing your home to 50,000 plus Home Optima App and Website owners.

Step 5

Market Response & Showing Feedback

On a daily basis we communicate with you to keep you updated on the progress of the sale.

Step 6

Negotiate

Our goal is not only to get you the highest possible dollars for your home but also make sure that the buyer is fully qualified and the contract terms are in your favour such that the deal closes without hiccups.

Step 7

Closing Support

We help you connect with the best lawyers in town, home inspectors, movers and the top mortgage agents. We continue to support you till the closing date and beyond for any help that you may need.
