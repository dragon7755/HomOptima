The homeoptima story

HomeOptima was built out of a love for finding families their perfect home. After their personal frustrating experience of home-search, our founders realized that there was a hunger in the market for a company that could provide a hassle free, seamless end-to-end experience. Leveraging technology and human experience, HomeOptima has made the journey of buying or selling a home stress-free and more transparent.

Founded in 2012, with a vision of improving the quality of homebuying decisions, HomeOptima soon became one of the most trustworthy names in house search as it dealt with every aspect of the consumers’ needs in the real estate industry.

Whether you are buying or selling, our goal is to help you understand the market and the true underlying value of home you are willing to buy or sell. We do this via our deep industry knowledge and unique proprietary software that allows us access to more data points than any other realty firm in the GTA - including AI based home valuations and strategies for winning multiple offers.

We also go the extra mile by offering professional services that cover every aspect of a real estate transaction. That way, whether you need a mortgage broker, a home stager, a general contractor, or a home inspector, we have the connections that provide for a seamless buy/sell experience.

With the ever-evolving online search behavior, HomeOptima shares updated information pertinent to real estate activities, assisting prospective buyers and sellers to make informed buying decision. Keeping the interest of our consumers at its core, we also publish market stats and directional insights daily. Our home value tracking tool let's homeowners keep track of their property's value daily.

Our Mission

To be the preferred choice for our end consumers and real estate partners in their journey of discovering, buying, selling, renting, and financing a home. We do this by combining knowledge, data, and technology, and above all the passion of our people.
