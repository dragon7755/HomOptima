How it works - home buying with homeoptima

Our Proven 7-Step Home Buying Process

Duration 4-12 weeks

Step 1

Requirements Gathering Consultation. Free!

We listen to your situation and understand your home search requirements

Step 2

Mortgage Pre-Approva

We work with top mortgage brokers to get you pre-approved with 48 hours.

Step 3

Our Recommendations

We supply you a list of neighghbourhoods and home configurations that are the best fit for your needs. We also supply you with sample homes for sale meeting your criteria. We use our proprietary neighbourhood matchmaker tool.

Step 4

Market Discovery

We take you out to show homes from step 3. We transition your online search to in-person showings. It helps refining your requirements, prioritizing trade-offs and validating your preferences.

Step 5

Showings and Fine Tuning Search

We make showing trips, discuss trade-offs using our Showing Optimizer Tool to discover your potential home.

Step 6

Offer Planning & Securing the Property

We conduct a thorough market value analysis of the property, formulate an offer strategy, understand the seller's motivation, anticipate and monitor the # of offers to secure the property for you.

Step 7

Closing Support and Move In Services

We connect you to top industry experts in the areas of mortgage, lawyers, home inspectors, renovators, interior designers, movers and much more. Our service includes a complimentary professional deep cleaning of your new homes before you move it. Check our range of services in the services page
