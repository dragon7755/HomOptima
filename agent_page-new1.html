About Rachel

Dedicated, principled, compassionate, and relentless, Rachel has consistently demonstrated exceptional results for her clients, whether buyers or sellers, throughout all of Manhattan and the surrounding boroughs. Following over 25 years in her own retail business, Rachel has a keen sense of people, what makes sense and what brings a transaction to fruition. She is a skilled negotiator, with an unwavering commitment to business ethics and draws on a wealth of resources to support her clients well beyond their expectations. Rachel is multilingual and is fluent in Farsi, Arabic, and Spanish. Her multicultural background allows her to engage personally with people from all over the world while familiarizing them with the wonders of New York life. With diligence, market insight, and tenacity, Rachel has worked with developers, high-end sellers, and first-time buyers and renters with the same personal attention to ensure what is the right fit for their needs. A longtime resident of the Upper East Side, Rachel previously lived in Southern California, where she received her degree in Psychology and a Masters in Social Work from UCLA. When not attending to business, Rachel enjoys spending time with her two daughters, traveling, and exploring all the offerings of New York.
